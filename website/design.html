Security First

The Rust Core Vault

Our core engine is written strictly in Rust to establish absolute, unbreakable folder safety:

  • Built-In Folder Jail: Walls the app strictly inside its own single directory (a sandbox lock). This makes "folder hacking" (unauthorized path traversal) mathematically impossible—your other directories are 100% physically invisible to the app.
  • Flawless Stability: Built from the ground up to prevent memory leaks, security exploits, and random crashes.
  • Instant Scanning: Scans massive nested folders instantly and safely with zero lag.
Direct Delivery

Direct Peer-to-Peer Highways

No cloud servers, no corporations storing your files, and no artificial speed limits. SEYFR connects you directly.

  • Direct Hole-Punching: Pokes a secure, temporary tunnel (NAT traversal) through strict home firewalls to link your devices directly with zero middlemen.
  • Perfect Delivery: Automatically checks every single block of data to guarantee your files arrive uncorrupted.
  • Unlimited Speed: Transfer files as fast as your direct physical internet connection allows.
